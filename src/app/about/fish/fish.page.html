 Basic Recipe

Start with raw, thawed, & cooled fish

Cut into cooking-size pieces

Cut 3-4 slits halfway down through the top of the salmon

Season top with salt, olive oil

Season bottom skin with pepper, olive oil

Put frying oil in pan and get it hot

Start salmon skin-down and fry until 3/4 of the side is light pink

Flip & cook the top for a few minutes

Get the sides cooked & crispy and add any extra seasons like basil

EAT
